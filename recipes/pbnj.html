Peanut Butter and Jelly

A traditional sandwhich consisting of Peanut Butter and Jelly, spread across white bread

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly (Prefereably Grape)
  • Bread (Prefereably White)

  1. Spread Jelly on two slices of bread
  2. Spread Peanut Butter on the same two slices of bread
  3. Place each piece of bread on top of each otherm spread with the spread on either side touching.
